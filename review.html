The Ol' Bourbon

"My own special cure-all"

by Sharon Wainwright

SCORE

When you get to be my age, there's nothing but blistering headaches from your kids screaming about their counter strikes and their mining and crafting. The cure to your manic migraine? No it isn't Paracetamol, it's a good bourbon!

These bad boys pack a mean punch with a brilliant brown color and a hearty tang, there truly is no better drink. Just make sure the kids can't get to it.
