Help Section

Biscuit bash is a spin on the renowned "Cookie Clicker" game. Simply click on the biscuit in the center of the screen and watch as the counter increases.

After reaching certain goals, you'll unlock new biscuits and power ups! These power ups will help boost your counter even faster. Certain biscuits have unique properties, too! Try out different biscuit/upgrade combinations with the side panels!

There are 12 unlocks to collect, try to get to 77,777,777 clicks as fast you can!
