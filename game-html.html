Help Section

Biscuit bash is a biscuity spin on the infamous "Cookie Clicker" game. Simply click on the cookie in the center of the screen and watch as the counter increases.

After reaching certain goals, you'll unlock new biscuits and power ups! These power ups will help boost your counter even faster. Certain biscuits have unique properties, too so no one biscuit will be all powerful, try out different combinations with the side panels!
